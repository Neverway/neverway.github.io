    Description

    Fadeout: Underground Report is a story driven cooperative third-person shooter.
    This is a free fan-made project based on the retro-style arena shooter Fadeout: Underground created by Pandan.

    Deep underground, the mysterious organization Orchard oversees a mining operation to expand the civilization that had taken refuge away from the destroyed world of the surface long ago. One day, the miner Camille begins to discover that Orchard may be hiding the real reason everyone is stuck here.
