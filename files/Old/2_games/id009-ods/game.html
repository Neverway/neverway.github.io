About

VR-O.D.S is a team-based virtual reality game where an inter-planetary delivery group works together to get their mothership across a hostile galaxy in one piece. Through your journey you will have to fend off invaders, maintain the mothership and fighters, and upgrade the ships with guns and defenses.

Development status: ON HOLD

Project Version: Private Testing [G4.0.0]
