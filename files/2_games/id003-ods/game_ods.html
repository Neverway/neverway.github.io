About

VR-O.D.S is a team-based virtual reality game where an inter-planetary delivery group works together to get their mother-ship across a hostile galaxy in one piece. Through your journey you will have to fend of invaders, maintain the mother-ship and fighters, and upgrade the ships with guns and defenses.
