About

VR-O.D.S is a team-based virtual reality game where three classes work together to get their mothership across a hostile galaxy in one piece. Through your journey you will have to fend of invaders, maintain the mothership and fighters, and upgrade the ships with guns and defenses. In VR-O.D.S there are three classes, offensive, defensive, and support.

Offensive - The offensive's job is to pilot the fighters and man the turrets to fend off any invaders of the motherships path. They will have to routinely return to the mothership for fuel, repairs, and upgrades.

Defensive - The defensive's job is to keep the mothership in good condition, as well as fending off any un-welcome guests. They will need to perform tasks around the ship such as repairing breaches, solving wiring problems, and navigating the mothership to it's course.
