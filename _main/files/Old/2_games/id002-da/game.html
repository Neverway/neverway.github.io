About

Divine Annihilation is a choose-your-own-adventure, story based boss-rush game that follows a group of friends as they try to uncover the mysteries of their pasts. In this mind bending twist on the classic tale of light vs dark, fight your way through puzzles, enemies, and bosses to reveal the truth.

Development status: ACTIVE

Project Version: Private Testing [G13.0.0]
