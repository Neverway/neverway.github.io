    Description

    Corridor Geodesic is a steampunk style puzzle game that offers a unique experience catering to newcomers and puzzle veterans alike. This game is designed to give players a new way to approach how they interact with, and manipulate, the environment around them.

    Descend through the floors of an underground research facility, built on the bones of an abandoned copper mine, as you try to reach and destroy the central core powering the complex.
